    Biography

    Cassandra Ortiz was born and raised in Buffalo, NY. Being from a large extended family has always brought out her outgoing side. Through the years, she became restless and needed a change.

    After college, she moved to Atlanta, GA where she started her family and developed her career in construction. As a Full-Time Working mother, she has learned her true strength and perseverance as a woman. Rising to become a Manager within her company, she earned the respect of her fellow colleagues.

    In 2017, Cassandra relocated to Seattle, WA for her husbands job with Amazon. She continues to work remotely with her company, as well as volunteering her time in the afternoons to the local church and clothing bank.

    When she gets a moment for herself, she can get lost in the many craft projects she likes to take on.
